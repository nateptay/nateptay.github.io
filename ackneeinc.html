Acknee Inc.

Date: 3/22/2017-6/5/2017

My Role: Producer, Programmer

Project Type: Game

Languages/Engines/Tools: Zero Engine, ZilchScript

Working with a team of artists allowed me to focus on game features, instead of splitting my efforts with art and coding.

In this game, players can confront or try to evade attackers in space, while raising funds to assemble a rocket that can take them home. Oh, and there's a cyborg space horse that talks with aliens to find good deals on cookies and rocket pieces.
