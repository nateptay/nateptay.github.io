Mission Statement

The purpose of this resturaunt is to try something new. Throw away that silly rule that vegetables must be eaten before dessert. Everyone enjoys some variety in their lives and this is a fun reminder to loosen up and appreciate the little things in life like eating your dessert first!

Founding Information

Unbeknownst to most of mankind, this restaurant has been around for centuries. The concept was developed by the Mongols and Romulus and Remus founded the restaurant.
